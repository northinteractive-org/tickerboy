How this is calculated

Per-approach chance is the product of independent factors: single → open to dating → accepts your age → clears the height bar → venue receptive (adjusted for timing) → your delivery, then nudged by your own logged outcomes.

Numbers are directional estimates from public research (U.S. Census/ACS marital status, Pew Research on singles, OkCupid age-preference data, height-preference surveys). With a free Census API key added, the "single" factor is refined with real local data for your county. This is entertainment and a nudge toward what you control — not a guarantee.
