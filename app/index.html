How this is calculated

Your per-approach chance is the product of independent factors: single → open to dating → accepts your age → clears the height bar → venue is receptive → your delivery.

Numbers are directional estimates drawn from public research (U.S. Census/ACS marital status, Pew Research on singles, OkCupid age-preference data, height-preference surveys). Venue and delivery figures are estimates. This is entertainment and a nudge toward the factors you control — not a guarantee. Approach respectfully and read the room.
